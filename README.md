## Key Features:
- **Automated Two-Way Exchange**: Users can seamlessly buy or sell TF2 Keys and Tour of Duty (TOD) Tickets for Steam Gems using simple, intuitive commands (`!buytf`, `!selltf`, `!buytod`, `!selltod`).
- **Customizable Profit Spreads**: You have absolute control over the exact gem value for buying and selling assets. The bot automatically calculates the math and retains the difference, locking your profit securely in its inventory.
- **Dynamic Live Status & Stats**: The bot automatically updates its Steam Profile status to display its current bankroll, and users can instantly check available reserves using the `!stats` command.
- **Smart Inventory Engine**: Automatically checks user and bot inventories for sufficient funds before executing trades, including advanced logic to dynamically split and stack Steam Gems flawlessly.
- **Built-in Multi-Language Support**: Fully localized interactions supporting 5 languages (**English, Spanish, Chinese, Serbian, German**). Users can easily switch with the `!lang` command.
- **Admin Support & Ticketing System**: Users can send `!support` messages directly to the bot, and admins can seamlessly respond to them using the `!reply [SteamID]` command.
- **Transparent Financial Logging**: Beautiful, readable terminal logs provide admins with an exact financial breakdown of every transaction (detailing exactly what the bot gave vs. what it received). 
- **Standard Bot Protection**: Includes our trusted anti-spam protection, auto-accepting offline friend requests, automated inactive friend removal, and auto-inviting users to your Steam Group.
- **Detailed Chat & Trade Logs**: View all chat messages natively in the console, and log every accepted exchange automatically into cleanly organized text files for your business records.
- **Exceptional Stability & Support**: Backed by years of community-trusted development to ensure your currency exchange operations never go offline.
